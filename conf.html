Delivery

Stock items are usually despatched within 1-2 working days. Made to order products usually carry lead times of a few weeks, but please note every product is different. We encourage you to check the 'Delivery' section of each product you've ordered for accurate information.

Larger, made to measure garments normally have a minimum 6-8 week lead time unless you have selected our express delivery service. Some made to measure products come with a longer lead time up to 14 weeks. We will study your measurements very carefully and contact you if something seems incorrect.
